About Me

A marketer who truly understands customers

My career didn't start in marketing — it started with people. Years of customer-facing roles in banking, insurance, and tech gave me something most marketers don't have: a genuine understanding of what customers need, what questions they ask, and what makes them trust a brand.

That customer service mindset shapes everything I create. When I write copy, build a campaign, or plan a content strategy, I'm thinking about the real person on the other end — their hesitations, their goals, and how to meet them where they are.

I love storytelling, I embrace AI as a creative tool, and I'm always curious about smarter ways to connect brands with the people they serve.
